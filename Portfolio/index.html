| BIOGRAPHY |
Dale de Guzman is a digital artist based in Tracy, California. He began creating short-form video edits during high school, which sparked a lasting passion for digital media and storytelling. After earning his Associate of Arts degree in Digital Media from San Joaquin Delta College, he transferred to San Jose State University to further his education. In 2025, Dale graduated with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Digital Media Art from SJSU, where he deepened his expertise in video production, digital illustration, and multimedia design. His academic journey has shaped a creative practice that blends technical skill with narrative-driven content.

| ARTIST STATEMENT |
Rooted in storytelling and visual communication, Dale’s work blends creativity, emotion, and intention to create immersive digital experiences. His video and digital artworks explore themes of identity, memory, and transformation, often drawing from personal experiences and cultural observations. Through a careful balance of composition, pacing, and mood, Dale invites viewers into introspective spaces that speak to both individual and collective journeys. His creative process is driven by a desire to connect — to make others feel, reflect, and see the world through a more nuanced lens. By embracing vulnerability and experimentation, Dale continues to push the boundaries of visual storytelling, crafting work that is both deeply personal and widely resonant.
