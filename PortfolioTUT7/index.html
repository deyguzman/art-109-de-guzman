Dale de Guzman

| EDUCATION | Associate of Art in Digital Media, San Joaquin Delta College, Stockton CA. 2022. Bachelor of Fine Arts in Digital Media Arts, San Jose State University, San Jose CA. 2022-Present.

| BIOGRAPHY | Dale de Guzman is a digital artist based in Tracy, California. He is a multifaceted artist who specializes in video and started producing video artwork during his time in high school. Dale created short-form video edits in high school, prompting him to pursue a career in digital media art. After receiving his Associate of Arts degree in Digital Media at San Joaquin Delta College in Stockton, he transferred to San Jose State University during the Fall 2022 semester. He is pursuing his Bachelor of Fine Arts degree in Digital Media Arts.
| ARTIST STATEMENT | Dale specializes in videography in the digital art world. His practice is deeply rooted in storytelling and visual communication as he strives to capture the essence of moments, narratives, and emotions. Dale’s artworks are an opportunity for him to connect with his audience and explore the intertwining of creativity and personal expression as he aims to push the limits of visual storytelling. Dale’s artwork serves as a reflection of his journey throughout life as well as the digital art world. He incorporates the complexities of growth within his artworks to showcase a world of authenticity. I believe art should make you feel something — even if it's just a smile or a shiver.
